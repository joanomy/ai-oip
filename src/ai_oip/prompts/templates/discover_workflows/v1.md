Below are numbered problems extracted from real online discussions.
Identify the distinct, recurring business workflows that underlie
them — the processes someone performs (or fails to perform) that
produce these problems. A single workflow may explain several
problems; merge them.

Problems:

{{ problems_digest }}

Respond with JSON in exactly this shape:
{"workflows": [{"name": "...", "description": "...", "steps": ["..."], "actors": ["..."], "problem_indexes": [1]}]}

- name: a short, specific name for the workflow (e.g. "Vendor invoice data entry").
- description: what the workflow accomplishes and why it exists, one or two sentences.
- steps: the concrete steps a person performs today, in order.
- actors: the roles/people who perform or depend on this workflow.
- problem_indexes: the [n] numbers of the problems this workflow underlies.

If no recurring workflow can be identified, respond with {"workflows": []}.
